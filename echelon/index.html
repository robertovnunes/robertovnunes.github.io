Info

Just a echelon/reduce matrix calculator to Linear Algebra (~MA531) discipline, ministred by Professor Dr. Pedro Machado Manhães de Castro (pmmc@cin.ufpe.br), at Centro de Informatica (CIn) - UFPE

This aplication was developed with objective of help everyone that are studying Linear Algebra

Using EPS equals 10e-8 and five digits for decimal values.

Echelon/Reduce your matrix

Shows PA=LU

Has a system that shows step-by-step the operation

Provides Elementar Matrix at step-by-step
